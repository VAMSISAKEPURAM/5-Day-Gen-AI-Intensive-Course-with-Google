# 5‑Day Gen AI Intensive Course with Google (Kaggle Practice)

This repository contains my practice notebooks and files from the **5‑Day Generative AI Intensive Course** offered by Google on Kaggle.  
It documents my daily learning journey, experiments, and applied exercises in Generative AI.

---

## 📚 Course Outline

### Day 1: Foundational Models & Prompt Engineering
- Explore the evolution of **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, from transformers to fine‑tuning, inference acceleration, and reasoning models.  
- Practice the art of **prompt engineering** for optimal LLM interaction.

### Day 2: Embeddings and Vector Stores/Databases
- Learn the conceptual foundations of **embeddings** and **vector databases**.  
- Study embedding methods, vector search algorithms, trade‑offs, and real‑world applications with LLMs.

### Day 3: Generative AI Agents
- Build sophisticated **AI agents** by understanding their core components.  
- Explore iterative development processes and recent **agentspace** advancements.

### Day 4: Domain‑Specific LLMs
- Delve into specialized LLMs like **SecLM** and **Med‑PaLM**.  
- Gain insights from researchers who built these domain‑specific models.

### Day 5: MLOps for Generative AI
- Adapt **MLOps practices** for Generative AI.  
- Leverage **Vertex AI tools** for foundation models and generative AI applications.
